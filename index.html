Lennon Chaves received a Bachelor Degree in Computer Engineering from Federal University of Amazonas. Currently, he is a Masters Student at Federal University of Amazonas, researching in Control Systems and Systems Verification. In 2017, he was enrolled at Computer Science Department at University of Oxford, researching about Program Synthesis applied to Digital Controllers. Recently, Mr. Chaves attended the Computer-Aided Verification Conference by participating in the Verification Mentoring Workshop at Heidelberg, Germany. Previously, in industry, he worked as Systems Analyst at Fundação Paulo Feitoza. He had also studied at Fundação Nokia de Ensino (Currently known as Fundação Matias Machline), where he could learn about logic and programming during his High School. His interests are focused on the field of Automation and Systems Control, Systems Model Checking, Automated Testing, Program Synthesis, and Software Enginnering. [Full CV]
