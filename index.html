Lennon Chaves received a Bachelor Degree and Master Degree in Computer Engineering from Federal University of Amazonas, in 2014 and 2018, respectively. In 2017, he was enrolled at Computer Science Department at University of Oxford, researching about Program Synthesis applied to Digital Controllers. In 2017, Mr. Chaves attended the Computer-Aided Verification Conference by participating in the Verification Mentoring Workshop at Heidelberg, Germany. He had also studied at Fundação Nokia de Ensino (Currently known as Fundação Matias Machline), where he could learn about logic and programming during his High School. His interests are focused on the field of Software Testing, Systems Model Checking, Automated Testing, Program Synthesis, and Software Enginnering. Currently, Mr. Chaves is working as Software Test Engineer at CESAR, an Institute of Innovation in Brazil.[Full CV]
